About the Artist

Pranamita Roy, from a musically enriched family, is graded B‑High by All India Radio in classical and semi‑classical. She has mastery in Kirana and Gwalior gharanas, and is admired for her depth in Aalap, melodic vistaar, and swara stability. Beginning at age 3 with Smt. Dipti Mukhopadhyay, she expanded her training under Pt. Arun Bhaduri at ITC Sangeet Research Academy, followed by mentorship from Pt. Vinayak Torvi, and currently trains under Pt. Vishwanath Nakod in Bangalore.

Academic & Musical Credentials

  • Sangeet Bivakar – Bangiya Sangeet Parishad (West Bengal)
  • Sangeet Visharad with Distinction – Pracheen Kala Kendra (Chandigarh)
  • B.Tech in Electronics & Communication Engineering (Former Telecom Engineer at Ericsson)

Professional Highlights

  • Category Manager & Vocal Trainer – Artium Academy, Mumbai
  • Former Faculty – KaushikDhwanee Music Academy (Hyderabad), De‑Sales Music Academy (Bangalore)
  • Judge – Sarala Birla Academy, Bangalore
  • Released “Celestial Mood of Raagas” CD album
  • Performed at Rabindra Bhavan (Hyderabad), Sur‑Sangam Academy (Kolkata), International Music Conference (Andul), and across Bangalore, Mysore, Dharwad & more

Recognition & Performances

  • Winner – Multiple All Bengal classical music competitions
  • Praised by local media and music circles for emotive performances
  • Known for clarity in raaga exposition and graceful semi‑classical presentations including bhajans and thumris
